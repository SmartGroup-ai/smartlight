About Us

SmartLight.ai is developing a plug and play device that uses embedded artificial intelligence to autonomously control urban street lighting to optimize lighting levels based on the surrounding environment.

This project aims to address key urban challenges such as energy usage, running costs, light pollution, safety and nighttime economy.
